---------------------------------------------------------------Version 1-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Product: Water Level Controller with mobile control
Description: This product used as supporting device for existing water level AC sensing controller and adds extra features for user convenience.
Features: Mobile app control (Only motor on), Status in Mobile app, Power cut detection with backup power, Sump water status in percentage(ultrasonic sensor).
Items used: 3 controllers(2 NSK ESP,1 Node MCU), 2 channel 5V relay, mobile charger, 5V Fan, voltage divider circuit - resistors, Ultrasonic sensor, power bank.
